# 🎨 Moooscle — Brand Guidelines

This document defines the official visual identity, style, and communication
guidelines for the Moooscle project.  
It ensures consistency across all platforms, assets, and community creations.

---

## 🐮 Core Identity

Moooscle is a character‑driven brand built on:

- strength  
- discipline  
- playful surrealism  
- community belonging  
- visual clarity  

The mascot is the emotional anchor of the project.  
Every asset must reinforce Moooscle’s personality and recognizable silhouette.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

### **Primary Colors**
- **Polygon Purple** — `#8247E5`  
  Used for outlines, accents, and brand identity.
- **Moooscle Blue‑Violet Glow** — `#6A5CFF`  
  Used for lighting, glow effects, and emphasis.
- **Pure White** — `#FFFFFF`  
  Used for contrast, clarity, and clean composition.

### **Secondary Colors**
- **Deep Shadow Blue** — `#1A1F3C`  
  Used for depth, shading, and dramatic contrast.
- **Soft Sky Blue** — `#A8C7FF`  
  Used for highlights and soft gradients.

The palette must remain **clean, bold, and high‑contrast**.

---

## 🔤 Typography

### **Official Moooscle Font (Titles & Banners)**  
A **bold sans‑serif rounded uppercase** font with:

- thick letters  
- 3D illustrated effect  
- **Polygon‑purple outline**  
- **white border**  
- **blue‑violet external glow**  

This is the **only** style allowed for:

- banners  
- motivational posts  
- official graphics  
- character‑centric visuals  

### **Body Text (Documentation & Long Form)**  
Use simple, readable fonts such as:

- Inter  
- Roboto  
- Open Sans  

No decorative fonts for long text.

---

## 🐮 Mascot Guidelines

### **Anatomy Rules (non‑negoziabili)**

- No human skin  
- No extra limbs or extra ears  
- No distorted proportions  
- No off‑model faces  
- No realistic textures  
- No uncanny or horror variations  

Moooscle must always remain:

- cartoonish  
- friendly  
- strong  
- expressive  
- instantly recognizable  

### **Allowed Variations**
- outfits  
- accessories  
- themed environments  
- cultural adaptations  
- seasonal or event‑based versions  

### **Forbidden Variations**
- gore  
- horror  
- hyper‑realistic anatomy  
- sexualized content  
- political imagery  

---

## 🖼️ Asset Style

### **General Rules**
- High‑resolution PNG  
- Transparent background  
- Subtle shadow under the character  
- Clean edges  
- No pixelation  
- No watermark  
- No copyrighted logos (Pixabay compliance)

### **Composition**
- Moooscle must be the focus  
- Clear silhouette  
- Strong lighting  
- Bold colors  
- Minimal clutter  

---

## 📣 Communication Style

### **Tone**
- playful  
- disciplined  
- motivational  
- surreal but coherent

- promises

- diversity
